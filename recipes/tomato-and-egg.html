Tomato and Egg Stir-Fry

Description

This is a recipe for tomato and egg stir fry. A classic Chinese dish beloved by all.

Ingredients

  • Tomatos
  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Green Onions

Steps

  1. Chop tomatos and scramble eggs
  2. Heat pot and add oil and tomatos, start stirring
  3. Once well cooked, add eggs.
  4. Turn off the stove, stir in some chopped green onions.
  5. Enjoy!
