Miso Soup

Description

This is a recipe for miso soup. A classic Japanese soup typically served with a special meal.

Ingredients

  • Miso paste
  • Tofu
  • Wakame
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot
  2. When water is done boiling, add chopped tofu.
  3. As tofu cooks, dilute the miso paste in a bowl with water until it has a more liquid consistency.
  4. Turn off the stove, stir in the dried wakame and miso paste.
  5. Enjoy!
