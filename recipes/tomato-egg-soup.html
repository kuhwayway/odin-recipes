Tomato and Egg Soup

Description

This is a recipe for tomato and egg soup. A simple tasty soup.

Ingredients

  • Tomatos
  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Green Onions

Steps

  1. Boil water in pot.
  2. Add chopped tomatos.
  3. Once well cooked, turn off stove and stir scrambled eggs in quickly.
  4. Turn off the stove, stir in some chopped green onions and sesame oil.
  5. Enjoy!
